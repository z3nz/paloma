# Scout: AI Music Production Tooling (March 2026)

> **Context:** Adam is producing his first album "Magnum Opus" — 7 tracks, hip-hop/trap with dark cinematic, ethereal vibes (Travis Scott, Kendrick, Kid Cudi, A$AP Rocky production styles). He records his own rap vocals. Currently using Suno for beats, Audacity for editing. macOS M4 Max, 128GB RAM. Budget flexible.

---

## TL;DR — What to Do TODAY (Bottom Line Up Front)

**3 moves that will 10x your production speed immediately:**

1. **Buy Logic Pro ($200 one-time)** — It runs natively on M4 Max, has AI features built-in (Stem Splitter, Session Players, Mastering Assistant), and is the fastest path from idea → finished track on Mac. Never go back to Audacity.
2. **Subscribe to LANDR ($7/mo) + iZotope Nectar 4 (buy once, ~$149)** — LANDR automates mastering in 60 seconds after you finish a track. Nectar gives you AI vocal processing (pitch correction, harmonies, de-essing) with one click. Together they replace an entire professional workflow step.
3. **Keep Suno for beat ideation, but export 12-stem files** — Upgrade to Suno Pro ($10/mo) to get stem export. Import stems into Logic Pro so you can rearrange, replace kick drums, add your own layers on top. Stop using Suno as a finished product — use it as a sketch pad.

## Section 1: Conversational / Voice-to-Music AI Tools

These are tools where you describe what you want and AI generates or modifies audio.

### Suno v5 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Already using — upgrade it)
- **What it is:** Text-to-song. Describe your beat, it generates full instrumentals (and AI vocals you don't need).
- **Key upgrade needed:** Suno Pro gives you **12-stem exports** and v5 model (highest fidelity). Without stems you're stuck with a sterile stereo mix you can't work with.
- **Pricing:** Free (50 credits/day, v4.5) | Pro $10/mo (2,500 credits, 12-stem export, commercial use, v5)
- **For Adam:** Use it to sketch vibes fast. Describe "dark trap, melodic, minor key, epic cinematic, atmospheric reverb, Sub bass heavy, 140 BPM" and iterate. Export the stems and rebuild in Logic.
- **Limitation:** The AI vocals are filler — mute them. The instrumentals are the valuable part.

### Soundverse AI ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Best conversational AI music tool)
- **What it is:** Full AI music platform with **SAAR** — a conversational voice AI assistant you literally talk to. You speak: "Make me a dark trap beat with 808s and cinematic strings." It generates, you refine conversationally.
- **Key differentiator:** Voice-to-music workflow. The most "talk to the AI" experience in the space.
- **Features:** Text-to-music, voice assistant (SAAR), stem separation, music extension, lyric generation, custom AI DNA models
- **Pricing:** Freemium, paid plans from ~$12.49/mo
- **For Adam:** Best for his stated goal of "talking to an AI" to describe his vision. SAAR is genuinely conversational.
- **URL:** soundverse.ai

### Udio ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Strong competitor to Suno, currently limited)
- **What it is:** AI music generation with outstanding vocal realism and cohesive harmonies
- **Sonic signature:** Cleaner lead vocals, smoother mixes than Suno. More "studio-grade" feel.
- **December 2025 status ⚠️:** Udio signed a licensing deal with Universal Music Group. **Downloads and stem exports are currently DISABLED**. You can generate and listen, but can't export WAV files right now. Major blocker for production use.
- **Credit limits post-deal:** Standard ($10/mo) = 2,400 credits; Pro ($30/mo) = 6,000 credits
- **For Adam:** Worth monitoring. Once exports return in 2026, Udio may be the best tool for final-quality instrumental references. Right now: use Suno instead.

### SOUNDRAW ⭐⭐⭐½
- **What it is:** Royalty-free beat generator optimized for Hip-Hop, Trap, Lo-Fi. Genre presets + customizable elements.
- **Key feature:** You pick mood, genre, length, and it generates royalty-free stems you can customize
- **Pricing:** ~$16.99/mo (creator); or pay-per-use
- **For Adam:** Good secondary option for quick instrumental sketches. Less conversational than Soundverse.

## Section 2: AI Beat/Instrumental Creation

Beyond Suno — tools for generating beats and layering sounds.

### Option 1: Suno Pro (already covered above) — primary beat tool

### Option 2: SOUNDRAW — royalty-free beats with customization
- Genre presets for trap, lo-fi, hip-hop, phonk
- Adjust: mood, tempo, instruments, energy level

### Option 3: Splice ($10.99/mo) — sample-based beat building
- **Not pure AI generation, but AI-assisted:** Huge library (100M+ samples) with AI-powered search
- Type in "dark trap 808" and it surfaces relevant samples
- New **Splice INSTRUMENT** plugin — play their entire library as a VST inside Logic/FL Studio
- AI stem splitter built in — upload any audio, split it into usable samples
- **Best for:** Building beats from real samples (the authentic way Travis Scott/Rocky producers work)
- **For Adam:** Splice is the industry standard for sample-based production. Essential.

### Option 4: iZotope RX 11 — Audio repair for samples
- If you're sampling from records or other sources, RX 11 removes noise, crackle, hum
- AI-powered click removal, dialogue isolation, spectral repair
- **Pricing:** ~$399 (Elements $99)

### Opinion: The Pro Workflow
Real trap producers (Metro Boomin, Mike Dean, WondaGurl) use a hybrid:
1. Start with a sample or a synth melody idea
2. Chop/layer in their DAW
3. Program 808s and hi-hats manually
4. Add samples from Splice or their own library

AI generators like Suno work best as **inspiration/reference**, not as finished instrumentals. Use them to find the vibe, then reconstruct the beat yourself (or with a producer) in Logic/FL Studio.

## Section 3: AI Vocal Processing

Adam records his own vocals. These tools handle everything after the raw recording.

### Antares Auto-Tune 2026 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Industry Standard)
- **The tool.** Used in literally every commercial hip-hop/trap record.
- **New in 2026:** Redesigned interface, 35% better pitch accuracy at 48Hz, 2.3x faster CPU processing, real-time visual feedback, presets from Zedd, DJ Swivel, Major Seven
- **Two modes:** Modern (natural-sounding correction) vs Classic (T-Pain hard-tune effect). Travis Scott signature sound = Classic mode at fast speed
- **Pricing:** $300 perpetual | **$249.99/year for Auto-Tune Unlimited (all 20+ Antares tools)** — subscription is better value
- **macOS:** Fully compatible, M4 native
- **For Adam:** This is non-negotiable. Get this. The Auto-Tune Unlimited subscription gives you Auto-Tune + Harmony Engine (automatic harmonies) + Mic Mod + more.

### iZotope Nectar 4 ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (AI Vocal Suite)
- **What it is:** All-in-one vocal processing plugin suite with AI
- **Key AI features:**
  - **Vocal Assistant:** AI analyzes your vocal, sets all parameters automatically as a starting point
  - **Voices module:** Instant vocal layers/harmonies with one click — no voice-leading knowledge needed
  - **Backer module:** AI background singers that sit behind your main vocal
  - **Auto-Level:** Like a recording engineer automatically managing your vocal levels (beats traditional compression)
  - **Breath Control:** Removes unwanted breath sounds automatically
  - **Audiolens:** Tone-match your vocal to any reference track
  - Includes **Melodyne 5 essential** for detailed pitch editing
- **Pricing:** Standard ~$149 | Advanced ~$349 | Also available via Splice rental $9.99/mo (Standard) / $19.99/mo (Advanced)
- **For Adam:** Use Vocal Assistant to get 80% there in seconds, then tweak. Combined with Auto-Tune, this is a complete vocal chain.

### Waves Vocal Production Bundle ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- Industry-standard plugins: Waves Tune (pitch correction), CLA Vocals (vocal chain presets), Renaissance Vox, Chris Lord-Alge packs
- **Buy during sales** — Waves plugins regularly go on sale for 70-90% off. Individual plugins can be $20-30 during sales.
- **For Adam:** Supplement to Nectar. CLA Vocals is great for quick "make it sound like a hit" one-knob processing.

### SynchroArts VocAlign ⭐⭐⭐½
- AI-powered vocal alignment — when you have double-tracked vocals or ad-libs, VocAlign makes them snap together in timing automatically
- **Pricing:** ~$199-349

## Section 4: AI Mixing & Mastering

### Mastering Services (Online / Cloud)

#### LANDR ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Best for Adam's workflow)
- **How it works:** Upload your stereo mix → AI masters it in 60 seconds → download streaming-ready master
- **AI analysis:** Analyzes your track, applies genre-appropriate EQ, compression, stereo widening, limiting
- **Reference tracks:** Upload a reference track (a Travis Scott song) and LANDR matches its sonic character
- **Album mastering:** All tracks get consistent mastering treatment across the album
- **Mobile app available**
- **Pricing:** Free previews | Plans from $6.58/mo (2 masters/mo) → $14.99/mo (unlimited)
- **For Adam:** Upload your rough mix, get a master in 60 seconds. For first-pass review and demos, this is invaluable. For final release, use it as a first pass and compare against human mastering.

#### Masterchannel ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Grammy-trusted AI)
- Used by Grammy-winning producers
- Streaming platform optimization built-in (hits Spotify/Apple Music loudness targets)
- Up to 192kHz/64bit exports
- Simple drag-and-drop interface
- **Pricing:** Unlimited free previews | From $15/mo
- **For Adam:** Strong alternative to LANDR. Different sound character — test both on your tracks.

#### RoEx Automix ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Best for stem mixing)
- **Key differentiator:** Takes **multiple stems** (vocals, drums, bass, instruments) and mixes + masters them — not just mastering a stereo file
- Upload your Logic Pro stems → AI handles the mix + master
- Genre-specific processing (has hip-hop modes)
- Reference track matching
- Mix reports with analytics
- **Pricing:** Free plan | ~€5.99/download for individual tracks
- **For Adam:** Once you have stems from Logic Pro, upload to RoEx for an AI mix. Compare the AI mix vs what you do manually — you'll learn a ton.

#### Cryo Mix ⭐⭐⭐⭐ (Conversational mixing)
- **Key differentiator:** Accepts stems + lets you guide the sound through **natural language** — "make the bass heavier, push the vocals forward"
- Not just one-click presets — you can iterate conversationally
- Specifically built for hip-hop, rap, R&B
- 100,000+ artists and producers
- **Pricing:** Free previews, no credit card required
- **For Adam:** This matches his stated desire to "talk to an AI" for his production workflow. Upload stems, describe your vision, iterate.

### In-DAW AI Mastering

| AI features | Most advanced | Few | None |

**Logic Pro's built-in AI features (M4 Max REQUIRED — you have it):**
- **Stem Splitter** — take any audio file (a Suno instrumental), split it into vocal/drums/bass/other stems. Then rearrange them.
- **Session Players** — AI musicians that play realistic drums, bass, and keys that follow your chords. For building backing tracks or sketching arrangements.
- **Mastering Assistant** — one-click AI mastering built into Logic itself
- **ChromaGlow** — AI harmonic saturation (makes things sound "analog warm")
- **Chord ID** — analyze chord progressions from any audio
- **Sound Library AI search** — natural language search: "dark ethereal pad" → instant results

**Also consider: FL Studio ($199 Producer) as a secondary beat tool**
- Better step sequencer and piano roll for 808s and trap drum programming
- Many top trap producers (Metro Boomin era) use FL
- Mac native, free lifetime updates
- **Recommendation:** Learn Logic first. If you find yourself wanting faster drum programming, add FL Studio later.

**What to avoid:**
- **Audacity** — not a DAW, it's an audio editor. Too limited for production. Graduate immediately.
- **Pro Tools** — industry standard for recording studios, but overkill and subscription-based. Wrong tool for your workflow.
- **GarageBand** — free on your Mac right now. Use it to learn while waiting for Logic. Same project file format — opens directly in Logic when you upgrade.

## Section 7: End-to-End Recommended Workflow

**The Adam Workflow (2026 AI-Assisted Hip-Hop Production)**

### Phase 1: Idea & Vibe (30-60 minutes per track)

```
1. Open Suno Pro (web browser)
2. Describe the vibe:
   - "Dark trap instrumental, 140 BPM, minor key, atmospheric reverb, 
     heavy 808 bass, cinematic strings, ethereal pads, Travis Scott 
     Astroworld energy, no vocals"
3. Generate 3-5 variations, pick the direction you like
4. Export 12-stem package from Suno
5. Import stems into Logic Pro
```

### Phase 2: Beat Construction in Logic Pro (1-3 hours)

```
1. Import Suno stems into Logic Pro
2. Use Logic's Stem Splitter to further isolate elements
3. Rebuild/enhance key elements:
   - Keep what sounds good (atmospheric pads, strings)
   - Replace kick/snare/808 with Splice samples (more control)
   - Use Logic's Drummer (Session Player) for hi-hat patterns
4. Program 808 bass line in Logic's Piano Roll:
   - Draw long notes, use pitch slides for glides
   - Add portamento for smooth Travis Scott-style 808 runs
5. Add your own melodic elements (Serum 2 for synth pads/leads)
6. Arrange: Intro → Verse → Hook → Verse → Hook → Bridge/Outro
7. Rough mix: balance levels (don't overthink — Auto-Level in Nectar will help)
```

### Phase 3: Vocal Recording (1-2 hours per track)

```
Hardware needed:
- Condenser microphone (Shure SM7B $400 or Audio-Technica AT4040 $299)
- Audio interface (Focusrite Scarlett Solo $120 or Focusrite Scarlett 2i2 $180)
- Pop filter ($15) + mic stand ($30)

Recording in Logic Pro:
1. Set up vocal track, arm for recording
2. Use Logic's Low Latency Monitoring 
3. Record multiple takes (Logic's "Takes" system stacks them)
4. Use Comp mode to pick the best lines from each take
5. Record doubles and ad-libs on separate tracks
```

### Phase 4: Vocal Processing (30-60 minutes)

```
Signal chain (in Logic Pro):
1. iZotope Nectar 4 → Vocal Assistant → click "Assist" → auto-setup
2. Adjust: de-esser intensity, compression, EQ to taste
3. Auto-Tune 2026 → Classic mode for that hard-tune effect
   OR Modern mode for subtle correction
4. Add reverb: Valhalla Supermassive (short dark room) on main vocal
   + Valhalla Supermassive (long ethereal plate) on sends
5. Add doubles: second vocal track slightly detuned, panned L/R
6. Ad-libs: separate track, more reverb, lower in the mix
```

### Phase 5: Mixing (1-2 hours)

```
In Logic Pro:
1. iZotope Neutron 5 on mix bus → Auto Mix → AI suggests settings
2. Manually balance: drums, 808, pads, lead vocals, doubles, ad-libs
3. Sidechain compress: kick/808 to create "pumping" feel
4. EQ with FabFilter Pro-Q 3: cut harsh frequencies, boost presence
5. Reference mix against a similar song (Sicko Mode, Pursuit of Happiness)
6. Check mono compatibility (fold to mono, nothing should disappear)
```

### Phase 6: Mastering (15-30 minutes)

```
Option A: Logic's Mastering Assistant (fastest)
- Apply on master bus
- AI analyzes and applies mastering
- Adjust loudness target to match streaming standards

Option B: iZotope Ozone 12 (most control)
- Use Master Assistant → AI creates signal chain suggestion
- Review each module, adjust to taste  
- Set limiter to -1.0 LUFS ceiling for streaming

Option C: LANDR (external, after bouncing mix)
- Export WAV from Logic at 24-bit/44.1kHz
- Upload to LANDR
- Upload a reference track (favorite Travis Scott song)
- Download mastered version in 60 seconds
```

### Phase 7: Release Prep

```
1. Check final master on multiple speakers:
   - Studio monitors (or good headphones initially)
   - AirPods/earbuds (most listeners)
   - Phone speaker (worst case scenario)
2. Upload to DistroKid ($22/yr — unlimited releases) or TuneCore
3. Set release date 2-4 weeks out for playlist submission window
4. Submit to Spotify Editorial Playlist (via Spotify for Artists) — 7+ days before release
```

## Key Risks & Watchouts

| Risk | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Suno/Udio copyright** | Both in copyright litigation. Suno settled with Warner Music Group for 2026. Udio settled with UMG. Licensed model usage (2026) should be safer, but the legal landscape is still evolving. Use for inspiration/production, not for releasing AI-generated instrumentals verbatim. |
| **Udio export disabled** | As of Dec 2025, Udio exports are disabled. Don't commit to Udio for current work. |
| **Logic Pro hardware req** | AI features (Stem Splitter, Session Players) require Apple Silicon (M4 Max = perfect). |
| **Auto-Tune hard-tune sound** | The Travis Scott hard-tune effect requires Auto-Tune Classic mode at retune speed 0. Make sure you get the real Antares plugin, not a knockoff. |
| **AI mastering vs human** | AI mastering is excellent for demos, release-ready work at the $10-20 level. For a major label submission or vinyl release, budget $200-500 for a human mastering engineer. For Magnum Opus as an album release, consider LANDR for online platforms + human engineer for the "official" physical/vinyl master. |
