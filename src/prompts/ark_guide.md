# ARK: Survival Evolved Guide

## Beginner's Guide
1. **Get Started**: Gather food, make a pickaxe, hatchet, and spear.
2. **Build a Base**: Start with a thatch foundation and storage boxes.
3. **Tame Dinos**: Use basic gathering dinos like Ankylosaurus, Beaver, and Dilogirus.
4. **Expand Your Base**: As you progress, build bigger structures to accommodate more resources and dino tames.
5. **Gather Resources**: Focus on key resource spots and explore the map for better yields.

## Intermediate Guide
1. **Upgrade Dinos**: Level up your tamed dinos for better performance.
2. **Craft Advanced Items**: Unlock and craft advanced tools and materials to increase efficiency.
3. **Explore New Areas**: Discover new maps and areas with unique resources and challenges.
4. **Defend Against Mobs**: Build defenses like fences, traps, and towers to protect your base from incoming threats.

## Advanced Guide
1. **Form a Dino Army**: Tame and breed dino armies for better defense and offense.
2. **Complete Caves**: Obtain artifacts by completing the cave systems on each map.
3. **Fight Sub-Bosses**: Challenge sub-bosses to gain rare resources and experience.
4. **Defeat Main Bosses**: Finally, defeat the main boss to unlock new areas and artifacts.
